Top 5 Healthy Habits

  1. Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily.
  2. Eat fresh fruits and vegetables.
  3. Exercise for 30 minutes a day.
  4. Get at least 7-8 hours of sleep.
  5. Practice stress-reducing activities like meditation.
